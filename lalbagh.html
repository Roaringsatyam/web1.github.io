Lalbagh Park: Bangalore’s Botanical Paradise

(Nature’s Beauty, History, and Tranquility)

Introduction:-

  • Bangalore’s Iconic Garden Landmark
  • Spread Across 240 Acres of Greenery
  • Home to Rare Plants and Species
  • Rich History, Cultural and Natural Heritage
  • Popular Destination for Nature Lovers
  • Popular Destination for All Ages
Explore more

History:-

  • Founded in 1760 by Hyder Ali
  • Original purpose as a botanical garden
  • Developed by Sri Chamaraja Wodeyar
  • Glass House inspired by Crystal Palace
  • Major site for scientific plant research

location:-

  • Near prominent landmarks like Bull Temple
  • Accessible via public transport and car
  • Close to Lalbagh Road and MG Road
  • Surrounded by residential and commercial areas
